About

Established in 2000, MKC Machine & Fab, Inc. is a Christian-owned full service machine and fabrication company. Founder and owner, Mike Smith, has 40 years of experience in metal working of all types. For nearly twenty years, it has been our goal to provide honest and dependable work.
